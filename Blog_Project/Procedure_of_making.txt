Step 3: Then Create apps for those individual app.
Step 4: Then Connect them into the Main project through the settings.
Step 5: Into the main project update the urls with the apps name.

Step 8: Then create super user  for the admin panel login.
Step 9: Then Make Migrations and Migrate the models.

Step 10: Make templates for each app and make a global templates and add it into the settings.py

Step 11: Inherit the base template into the each app template.
